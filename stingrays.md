# Stingray Facts
Stingrays are aquatic fish, that are made of only cartilage. Their flat bodies make it easy to hover the ocean flooe.
They are related closely to sharks, and are known to be dangerous because of the barb on their tails.
---
## Key Characteristics
- Flat Bodies
- Diamond shaped
- Flap their fins to swim
- Love to bury in the sand
- Some barbs on certain species are venomous
---
## Fun Fact!
- Stingrays aren't aggressive towards humans, when they "attack" they are normally stepped on or aggrivated in some way.
