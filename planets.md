# What planets are visible in Missouri in the Winter?
There are so many planets that can be viewed in the winter in Missouri during clear conditions.
Here are some planets that are visible only during December-February:
### Jupiter
- Bright
- Yellow/White Color
- Can be viewed almost all night

### Mars
- Red
- Can be viewed early morings in the late months of winter

### Saturn
- Yellow/Off-White
- Can be viewed in the early evening time
- You can see the rings with a small telescope

Make sure to avoid light pollution when viewing for best results!
